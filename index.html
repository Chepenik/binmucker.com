Want A Random Quote?

Binmucker (noun) - "A parent with an extraordinary talent for turning the most challenging and messy situations into learning opportunities for their children. Often mistaken for a magician, but really just a master of improvisation."

I am a writer, mediocre programmer, meme enthusiast, and professional shitposter. I've built this website as practice for learning how to code, and I'm sure my child will appreciate learning about what I've learned from raising her.

Whoever you are, anonymous visitor, I'm grateful that you are taking some of your most precious asset, time, and using it to visit my website. It brings me a lot of joy knowing there are others out there working towards self-sovereignty. It's one of the biggest lessons I hope to impart to my own child.

As a firm believer in the power of Bitcoin and other tools such as Nostr, I am convinced that they will play a vital role in creating a world of more sovereign individuals.

If you find value in my work, please consider supporting me by sending some sats to my address at chep@getalby.com. Additionally, I invite fellow Bitcoin enthusiasts to check out MassAdoption, a Boston-based startup focused on connecting Bitcoin enthusiasts through meetups and assisting companies in accepting Bitcoin by developing a web application and providing tech support. You can support our efforts by contributing to our Geyser fund.

Cheers!
